CLEOSPORTZ, established in 2023, is a sports marketing & management agency based in Kochi, India, having operations across the country. Realizing the growing popularity of sports and health related physical activities in the country and understanding the potential for brands in it, Cleosportz has come into inception.

We have started with the intention of establishing the synergy between sports, business, and communication in various media, with several firsts to its credit. Our association with reputed communication organizations has added a whole new dimension to our offering, enabling us to deliver 360-degree sports marketing and management solutions to the client.

We are a collection of visionaries, strategists, planners, and creators, brought together to deliver awe-inspiring experiences in events. We provide bespoke solutions that meet the objectives that are designed to help our clients engage and inspire their people, customers, and partners.
