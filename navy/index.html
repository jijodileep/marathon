    Kochi Navy Marathon

    In a remarkable initiative that blends physical fitness with naval camaraderie, the Kochi based Southern Naval Command recently unveiled its prestigious event, the 5th edition of Kochi Navy Marathon which will be conducted on 15th December 2024, marking a significant date in the maritime calendar. The marathon stands as a testament to the spirit of athleticism and discipline that defines the naval forces, encapsulating the ethos of the Indian Navy.
